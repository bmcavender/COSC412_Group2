Basketball Information

Health for Basketball

Basketball players thrive on carbs for energy and to do explosive movements like jumping and changing
direction. Most fruits and vegetables can provide this kinda of energy for the basketball athlete.
Carbs with a low glycemic index like beans, apples, and whole grains are ideal.
Proteins are also important to build muscle necessary for doing the explosive movements, and also for
recovery after intensive ball playing. As usual, lean meats are the healthiest and most nutritional for
athletes. Eat things like eggs, fish, or chicken. Whey protein
is also very good for supplemental protein if needed.

Fitness for Basketball

Always warm up your body with mobility exercises before playing or practicing. Some of the best exercises to do are high knees, lunges, and arm swings!

After a work out, cool down with a few stretches.
Yoga is a great way to relieve tension after a hard game. Try this sequence to loosen up.

Child's Pose
Down Dog
Spiderman Lunge
